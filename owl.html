A Wise Old Owl

There was an old owl who lived in an oak tree. Every day, he observed incidents
that occurred around him.
Yesterday, he watched as a young boy helped an old man carry a heavy basket.
Today, he saw a young girl shouting at her mother. The more he saw, the less he
spoke.
As the days went on, he spoke less but heard more. The old owl heard people
talking and telling stories.
He heard a woman saying an elephant jumped over a fence. He heard a man
saying that he had never made a mistake.
The old owl had seen and heard what happened to people. There were some who
became better, some who became worse. But the old owl in the tree had become
wiser, each and every day.
